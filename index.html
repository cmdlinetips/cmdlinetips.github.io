cmdlinetips.com

cmdlinetips.com is a blog that started in 2011 as a place holder for keeping tips on Linux/Unix. Slowly, there were more tips for using Python, and R for doing all things Data Science.

Initial idea for the website was simple. Just wanted a google-able How-To notes for personal use. Fast forward a few years.. then surprised to find a lot of traffic from outside, as the site has been useful for others as well. Now trying to keep adding some simple tips regularly. The website just hit a total of 300 published posts. Phew, that took like 11 years. Hurray!

Check out cmdlinetips.com
